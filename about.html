Find your new name

Find a new name that you are proud of without the binary BS of baby name lookups.Find yourself and have fun!

We've been there! You are transitioning and want a name to match your gender identity. Should it be short or long? More masc or more femme? Finally, you can pick a name that reflects who you are. How 'effin cool is that!?

u4ia will help you along this emotional ride by guiding you through our name directory for you to narrow down to your final choice of "Maybes". You can even get some of your closest friends and family involved and share your Maybe list with them.

Done picking your name? Now it's time to get to the dirty work. Court! Legal fees! The Social Security Administration! Ah! It can be scary, but chill out. Our handy checklist and state guidelines repository will make the process seamless.

You can do this! Now have fun and find yourself!
